Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about G V Green products and services

What payment methods does G V Green accept? ▼
G V Green accepts all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets including Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe. We also offer cash on delivery for orders below ₹5,000.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days, while express delivery takes 2-3 business days. For plants and live products, we use special shipping to ensure they arrive in perfect condition, which may take an additional day.
Can I return or exchange products? ▼
Yes, G V Green offers a 30-day return policy for most products. Plants can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they arrive damaged or unhealthy. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate a return or exchange.
Are your products really organic? ▼
Absolutely! All our organic products are certified by recognized authorities. We work directly with certified organic farmers and manufacturers to ensure the highest quality standards. Each product comes with proper certification information.
Do you provide gardening guidance? ▼
Yes, G V Green offers comprehensive gardening support through our blog, video tutorials, and expert consultation services. You can schedule a virtual consultation with our horticulture experts for personalized gardening advice.
Do you ship plants all over India? ▼
We ship plants to most major cities and towns across India. However, due to agricultural regulations, we cannot ship certain plant varieties to some states. Please check our shipping policy or contact customer service for specific location information.
